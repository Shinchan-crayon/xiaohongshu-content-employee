# 账号语气配置

账号语气由以下字段定义：

```yaml
audience:
tone: neutral | warm | sharp | professional
first_person: allowed | limited | forbidden
sentence_density: compact | balanced | relaxed
humor_level: none | light | moderate
preferred_words: [string]
forbidden_words: [string]
cta_style: question | suggestion | none
```

语气只改变表达方式，不改变事实。缺少配置时使用 `neutral + balanced + light`，不得自行虚构人格经历。
